The Door Store Privacy Policy

Last update 26. 10. 2020

This Privacy Policy explains how your personal data is collected and processed by Martin Krátký (“Developer”) when you use mobile game The Door Store (“App”).

The App is a free to play game (with optional in-game-purchases), and in order to maintain great game experience I process information from and about you in particular to improve the entire experience in the App and to support internal operations.

You will be notified every time this Privacy Policy changes by updating date at the top of this policy. You consent to this privacy policy by using App.

What data being collected?

Developer collects statistics about game such as number of completed levels or which characters being used mostly. Partners may collect personal data such as device identifiers, location data and other advertising statistics. No data is collected that could identify you, such as your name or date of birth.

What is use of collected data?

Developer use collected game statistics to improve players game experience such as improving levels that are difficult for players or replacement of unpopular characters. Partners use the data they collect to personalize ads. For more detailed information on the use of the collected data, I will refer you to the privacy policy of the partners:

Data is stored securely as you may read on the partners privacy policies.

Privacy rights

You have right to access or delete collected information. You can access or delete collected information by developer by clicking on “Open data privacy page” in App settings. For a way to obtain or delete data collected from partners, I will refer you to the partners' privacy policies
